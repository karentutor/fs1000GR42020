Brazil

Unique Things about Brazil

  • Brazil is the largest country in South America and the fifth largest nation in the world.
  • Brasilia, the country’s capital, took just 41 months to build, from 1956 to 1960 (Rio had been the capital for the previous 197 years).
  • Brazil has won the FIFA World Cup finals five times, more than any other nation.
  • Brazil's border touches all countries on the South American continent except Chile and Ecuador.

Argentina

Unique Things about Argentina

  • The name “Argentina” derived from the Latin word “silver”.
  • Argentina has the highest consumption of red meat in the world.
  • Perito Moreno Glacier is the third-largest freshwater source and also a glacier that is growing instead of shrinking.
  • Over 10% of the world’s flora is found in Argentina.
